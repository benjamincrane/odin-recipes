Lasagna

Description

Layers of sauce between sheets of pasta

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Fillings, if any, vegetables, meat, etc

Steps

  1. use fresh lasagna noodles or cook dry ones
  2. if any, cook fillings until ready to eat
  3. mix sauce and fillings together
  4. add a thin layer of sauce and fillings to lasagna pan
  5. add a thin layer of cheese on sauce
  6. add a layer of lasagna noodle
  7. repeat layers until the pan is nearly filled to the top, ending with cheese
  8. bake in the oven until done
