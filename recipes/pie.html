Pie

Description

A pastry exterior with tasty filling

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Fillings of choice: meat, fruit, anything edible

Steps

  1. Use flour and butter to make pie crust
  2. Shape pie crust in pie pan
  3. Put fillings in pie pan, on top of the crust
  4. Bake in oven
